Text Filter

 

Instructions

  1. Enter the document text you'd like to filter.
  2. Enter keywords or phrases to be filtered. Separate entries by comma or spaces. Pharses should be enclosed in quotation marks.
  3. (optional step) - Enter a symbol or phrase to replace each keyword/phrase with.
  4. Press 'Submit'

This is regular expression based and may contain errors. Check out the source on GitHub!
